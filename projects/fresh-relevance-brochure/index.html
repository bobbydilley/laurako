Fresh Relevance Brochure

Breif:

To produce a brochure to distribute to potential clients via post, email or at events, to promote Fresh Relevance and its features.

Solution:

I used clear spreads for each feature, mixing photography with our brand imagery - a scribble style of illustration with orange circles. This brochure was produced in A5 and half letter sizes for printing in the UK and US, and as a PDF for emailing.
